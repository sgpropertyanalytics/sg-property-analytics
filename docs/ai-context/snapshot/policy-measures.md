## ABSD (Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty)

**Effective**: 27 April 2023
**Source**: IRAS

| Buyer Profile | 1st Property | 2nd Property | 3rd+ Property |
|---------------|--------------|--------------|---------------|
| Singapore Citizen | 0% | 20% | 30% |
| Permanent Resident | 5% | 30% | 35% |
| Foreigner | 60% | 60% | 60% |
| Entity (non-housing developer) | 65% | 65% | 65% |

**Notes**:
- Rates apply to purchase price or market value, whichever is higher
- Married couples treated as single unit for property count
- Remission schemes may apply for specific circumstances

## LTV (Loan-to-Value) Limits

**Effective**: 20 August 2024 (HDB update), 16 December 2021 (private)
**Source**: MAS, HDB

### Private Property (Bank Loans)
| Loan | LTV Limit | Conditions |
|------|-----------|------------|
| 1st housing loan | 75% | Standard |
| 1st housing loan | 55% | If loan tenure >30 years OR extends past age 65 |
| 2nd housing loan | 45% | Standard |
| 2nd housing loan | 25% | If loan tenure >30 years OR extends past age 65 |
| 3rd+ housing loan | 35% | Standard |

### HDB Flats (HDB Loans)
| Loan Type | LTV Limit | Notes |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| HDB concessionary loan | 75% | Reduced from 80% in Aug 2024 |

**Notes**:
- Aug 2024: HDB LTV lowered from 80% to 75% to align with bank loans
- LTV applies to lower of purchase price or valuation
- Cash downpayment requirements apply beyond LTV
- CPF can be used up to Valuation Limit (VL)

## SSD (Seller's Stamp Duty)

**Effective**: 3 July 2025
**Source**: IRAS

| Holding Period | SSD Rate |
|----------------|----------|
| Up to 1 year | 16% |
| >1 year to 2 years | 12% |
| >2 years to 3 years | 8% |
| >3 years to 4 years | 4% |
| More than 4 years | 0% |

**Notes**:
- Holding period extended from 3 to 4 years (July 2025 update)
- Rates increased by 4 percentage points per tier to curb flipping
- Holding period starts from date of purchase (not completion)
- Applies to sale price or market value, whichever is higher
- Exemptions exist for specific circumstances (e.g., death, bankruptcy)

## BSD (Buyer's Stamp Duty)

**Effective**: 15 February 2023
**Source**: IRAS

| Property Value | BSD Rate |
|----------------|----------|
| First $180,000 | 1% |
| Next $180,000 | 2% |
| Next $640,000 | 3% |
| Next $500,000 | 4% |
| Next $1,500,000 | 5% |
| Above $3,000,000 | 6% |

**Notes**:
- BSD applies to all property purchases
- Calculated on purchase price or market value, whichever is higher
- Payable within 14 days of execution of agreement

## TDSR (Total Debt Servicing Ratio)

**Effective**: 16 December 2021
**Source**: MAS

- Maximum TDSR: **55%** of gross monthly income
- All debt obligations included (housing, car, credit cards, etc.)
- Medium-term interest rate floor: **4.0%** for floating rate loans

---

## MSR (Mortgage Servicing Ratio)

**Effective**: For HDB loans and EC purchases
**Source**: MAS

- Maximum MSR: **30%** of gross monthly income
- Applies to HDB flats and ECs purchased from developers
- Does not apply to private condo purchases
